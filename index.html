Denial of Service Attack

In this project, I demonstrate how a basic Denial of Service (DoS) attack works in a controlled lab environment. I show the tools used to overwhelm a target system and explain the concept of network flooding and system overload. This video was fully documented on my YouTube channel, where I walk through each step and explain how to safely test these techniques for educational purposes only.

Credential Harvesting

This project shows how credential harvesting can happen through social engineering techniques. Using Kali Linux and a phishing toolkit, I simulate a fake login page to collect user credentials in a test environment. I recorded and explained the entire process on YouTube, including the setup, execution, and why understanding these attacks is important for building better defenses.
